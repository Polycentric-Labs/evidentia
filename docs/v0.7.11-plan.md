## Theme

**Audit chain-of-custody + remaining governance primitives + v0.7.x
deferrals roll-up.** Closes the last bucket of items from the
v0.7.9 mega-release that didn't fit into v0.7.10's window, plus
the v0.7.10 minor refinements identified during the Pre-tag
review.

## P0 — Audit chain-of-custody + WORM

Retention metadata on `CollectionContext` + WORM backend support
across the three big-three cloud providers:

- **S3 Object Lock** (compliance + governance modes)
- **Azure Immutable Blob Storage** (time-based + legal-hold)
- **GCS Bucket Lock** (retention policy + locking)

Plus retention-policy lifecycle helpers (`evidentia retention
{set,verify,extend}` CLI verbs).

Estimated: 1.5 weeks.

## P1.5 G3 — KRI / KPI / KGI metric schemas

Pydantic schemas for the three risk-management metric
classifications:

- **KRI** (Key Risk Indicator) — leading metric warning of risk
  threshold approach
- **KPI** (Key Performance Indicator) — lagging metric measuring
  process effectiveness
- **KGI** (Key Goal Indicator) — outcome metric measuring
  achievement of the risk-management strategy

Plus a metrics dashboard CLI: `evidentia governance metrics
{add,list,report}` with threshold-alert hooks.

Estimated: 0.5 week.

## P1.5 G4 — Open FAIR risk quantification

Implement the Open FAIR (Factor Analysis of Information Risk)
taxonomy for dollarized risk quantification. New CLI: `evidentia
risk quantify --method open-fair --asset <asset> --threat <threat>`
producing dollarized loss-magnitude estimates.

Estimated: 1 week.

## P1.5 G5 — Process-as-code governance workflows

Structured governance workflow primitives — declare process
definitions in YAML/JSON, execute via `evidentia governance
workflow {run,status,log}`. Common patterns: change-approval
workflows, quarterly-review workflows, validation-cycle workflows.

Estimated: 1 week.
